FAQs




  • How long will it take to get my order?
    7-10 business days. Larger orders may take longer.
  • Can I get a rush order?
    We do not offer rush orders during the months of July, August, November, and December..
  • What are your business hours?
    We are open M-F 9-5:30. Closed for the weekend. Holidays may affect business hours.
  • Do you offer shipping?
    Yes! We offer domestic and international shipping.
  • Do you offer store pick up?
    Yes!
  • Do you accept returns?
    We do not offer refunds or returns.
  • What brands are your shirts and hats?
    We offer a variety of different shirts and hats to our customers. Feel free to browse the online catalogues, or bring in your own items.
  • Can I bring my own item or do i have to purchase through the company?
    You are welcome to purchase your own.
  • Can I get a custom made shirt done here?
    Absolutely! Bring in your ideas or send us an email. 1702@wbandm.com
  • Do you charge for custom designs?
    We do not charge for custom designs. There may be an art fee for digitizing or vectorizing artwork depending on the request of the order.
  • What if I just want one shirt?
    No problem!
  • What if I just want one hat?
    We can do that!
  • How large of an order can you produce?
    There's no such thing as an order too big.
  • Do you do sports jerseys?
    We do!
  • Can you add names to helmets?
    Yes!
  • Do you make patches?
    We are able to make patches.
  • Do you sew on patches?
    We can attach simple shaped patches.
