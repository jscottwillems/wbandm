Welcome to WB&M!

From One to Infinity... Every Job Displays Superior Creativity


WB&M is a small, local, family-owned business dedicated to creating your own custom orders. From the initial consultation, throughout the design phase and final production, our drive is to meet all customer needs and satisfaction. With Embroidery and Screen Printing being our most popular services, we also offer banners, thermal film and just about any imprinting that you would like on anything that you would like.

Why WB&M? We strive to:

  • Be attentive to detail
  • Produce impressive work
  • Be fulfilling of all of our customers demands
  • Consistently exceed expectations
  • Work as a determined and result oriented team

We are proud to offer:

Embroidery

We are the vehicle to your creativity. Choose from our multiple font options and stock designs, or bring in your own and we will digitize it to sew! This service is useful for company logos to be sewn on shirts, hats, bags; sport team hats and bags; school shirts; gifts and decor.

Screen Printing

A good choice for 15-1000+ items, all items receiving the same design. Unlimited colors, added colors do increase cost. Can print on any color with no added charge.

Thermofilm

A good choice for 1-20 items, can be personalized without extra cost. Limited to 1 to 2 color design. Can press on any color item with no added charge.

Transfer

A good choice for 1-20 items, can be personalized without extra cost. Unlimited colors, does fade over time. Can press on any color, dark colors do have an upcharge.

Decals

Want a vinyl sticker decal for your car, cup, notebook, window...we can do it!

Yard Signs

Corrugated signs are here! We can print unlimited color. Or choose a sign color and a vinyl color to have pressed.

Magnetic Signs

Typically 12" x 24" can be printed in unlimited color, durable.
